About Gull Family Orchards

At Gull Family Orchards, we are driven by family values, a love for farming, and a commitment to sustainable agriculture. Our journey is rooted in generations of hard work and the shared dream of cultivating the finest almonds in California.

Jacob Gull – Co-Founder

As a software engineer by trade, I’ve always had a passion for problem-solving and innovation. However, my heart has always been tied to nature and the land. After years of working in technology, I decided to pursue a lifelong dream of running my own farm and becoming a part of California’s rich agricultural tradition. With the guidance of my grandpa, a veteran and lifelong farmer, I’ve embraced the challenge of farming, blending modern techniques with time-honored practices to create a thriving orchard that reflects our family’s dedication to quality and sustainability.

Duane Gull – Co-Founder

A retired Marine and lifelong farmer, my grandpa brings a wealth of experience and wisdom to Gull Family Orchards. Having grown up working on a dairy farm and later serving his community as a Deputy Sheriff for Alemeda County for 31 years, he knows the value of hard work, discipline, and perseverance. His background in agriculture and his hands-on knowledge of farming have been instrumental in guiding our growth. Even in retirement, his passion for farming and dedication to family fuels our vision for the future.

Our Vision

At Gull Family Orchards, we believe in cultivating more than just crops. We’re committed to growing a family legacy, one that is rooted in tradition, sustainability, and a respect for the land. Our goal is to produce the highest quality almonds while caring for the environment, contributing to the local economy, and building a lasting legacy for future generations.
